ingredients

  • Shredded Chicken - 2 cups

    Softened Cream Cheese - 8oz

    Frank's RedHot Original - 1/2 cup

    Ranch Dressing - 1/2 cup

steps

  • PREHEAT over to 350F. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl. Spoon into shallow 1-quart baking dish.

    BAKE 20 minutes or until mixture is headted through; site. Sprinkle with green onions, if desired, and serve with chips, crackers and/or cut up veggies.
