FINNISH FETA PASTA

PREP TIME

10

MINUTES

COOK TIME

45

MINUTES

INGREDIENTS

―

1lb Cavatappi Noodles

Cherry tomatoes

Feta cheese

Cream cheese

Basil

Minced Garlic

Vegatble/Olive oil

DIRECTIONS

―

1.

Add noodles to a pot, full of water, and place the pot on the stove. Set eye to med-high and bring to a boil.

2.

Add Cherry Tomatoes to baking dish. Top with 1/2 cup of olive oil, kosher salt, pepper and toss thoroughly.

3.

Add Feta Cheese to the center of the baking dish containing the tomatoes. Add additional extra virgin olive oil, kosher salt, and pepper.

4.

Add baking dish to over, and cook at 400°F for 30 minutes.

5.

After Step 4, remove baking dish from oven and add garlic and basil. Mix thoroughly.

6.

Strain cooked noodles, from step 1, and add strained noodles to baking dish. Mix thoroughly.
