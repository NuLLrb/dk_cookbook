SHRIMP SLAW SANDWICH

PREP TIME

25

MINUTES

COOK TIME

30

MINUTES

INGREDIENTS

―

6 tbsp Greek Yogurt

6 tbsp Mayonnaise

1⁄4 cup apple cider vinegar

11⁄2 tbsp sugar

5 cups Broccoli slaw mix

24oz shrimp, peeled and deveined

3 tbsp Olive Oil

Salt

Black Pepper

6 Sandwich Rolls, toasted

11⁄2avocados, sliced

DIRECTIONS

―

1.

PREHEAT oven to 400°F

2.

MIX yogurt, mayonnaise, vinegar and sugar in a bowl. Add slax mix and stir to combine. REFRIGERATE until ready to serve.

3.

COAT shrimp with oil and season with salt and pepper. Place on baking sheet and bake until pink, about 3-4 minutes.

4.

Fill rolls with shrimp, slaw and avocados. SERVE.
