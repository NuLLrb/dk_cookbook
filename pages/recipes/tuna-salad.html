TUNA SALAD

PREP TIME

5

MINUTES

COOK TIME

5

MINUTES

INGREDIENTS

―

Canned Tuna

Mayonnaise

Celery

Parsley

Dijon Mustard

Salt and pepper

DIRECTIONS

―

1.

DRAIN canned tuna and add to mixing bowl.

2.

ADD mayonnaise, celery, parsley, mustard.

3.

MIX all ingredients in the bowl. Then season with salt+pepper.

4.

Serve!
